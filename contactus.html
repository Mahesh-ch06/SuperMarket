Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 6:00 PM.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in the "My Orders" section of your account. You'll also receive SMS and email updates with tracking information.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your fresh produce, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund or replacement.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders over ₹2000. Contact our sales team for custom quotes and business partnerships.
